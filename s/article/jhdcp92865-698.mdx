Invoca is the industry’s leading call tracking and analytics solution, helping marketers attribute inbound calls to digital campaigns. Google Campaign Manager is a web-based ad management system for advertisers and agencies, making it easy to manage digital campaigns across websites and mobile.  
  
This is the first article in a series that will teach you everything you need to know to set up, support, and succeed with the Invoca for Google Campaign Manager integration, including:   
 

- What’s possible with the integration
- Step-by-step directions to install your integration
- Help for marketers using the integration to view reports and create new audience lists
- FAQs and troubleshooting support

## Why choose Invoca for Google Campaign Manager?

Using these two products together, Invoca for Google Campaign Manager offers a complete view of the inbound call activity driven by your marketing efforts using [Google’s Offline Conversions API](https://developers.google.com/doubleclick-advertisers/guides/conversions_overview) (external link). With this integration, you can:  
 

- Report Invoca phone calls and Signals to Google Campaign Manager as Floodlight activities, to get a unified view of your campaign performance.
- Understand which marketing campaigns and ads are driving each type of call or Signal in Google Campaign Manager reports.
- Attribute your Invoca conversions to display ad views as well as  clicks, building a more accurate picture of how your ads are performing.
- Use your Invoca data to build Campaign Manager audiences, then leverage those audiences in your programmatic ad buying in Display & Video 360.
